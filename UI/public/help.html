How to Use:

  1. EEG Placement: Position the EEG electrode in the center of your forehead.
  2. EOG Placement: Place the EOG electrode above your eyebrow.
  3. Ground Placement: Place both of the ground electrode into your earlobe.
  4. Control Left/Right: EOG electrodes control left/right movements. Look right to turn right and left to turn left.
  5. Control Forward: EEG electrodes manage forward motion. Focus to move forward.
